Aloha Burgers

A delicious burger with a mixture of a spicy patty topped off with a sweet taste from the pineapple

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Cheyenne pepper
  • Pineapple
  • Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Buns

Steps

  1. 1lb Beef
  2. 1.5tbs of Garlic
  3. 2tbs of Cheyenne
  4. 1tbs of salt and pepper
  5. with all spices mix into a bowl with the beef and flatten patties once complete
  6. saute the pineapple then onions afterward and remove once complete then place beef into the pan to cook
  7. 1tbs of ketchup, relsih , and mayo mix into a small bowl
  8. once patty is done place the buns on the pan until toasted
  9. spread the sauce onto the toasted buns on both sides
  10. slice a piece or two of tomato and lettuce rinse then place on the bottom piece of bun
  11. place the patty next ont top the pineapple then onion the the top bun and enjoy.
