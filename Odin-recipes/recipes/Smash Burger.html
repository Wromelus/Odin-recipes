Smash Burgers

This is most deliciuos burger that has been made it's worth the calories on a cheat day

Ingredients

  • Garlic powder
  • 2lb beef
  • Buns
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Mushroom
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Honey
  • Salt and Pepper
  • stick of butter
  • Lettuce
  1. 1 tbs of Garlic, Salt, Pepper and Chyenne perpper into a bowl and mix>
  2. Mix Ketchup, Mayo, and Reslish too another bowl the amount of each is a prefrence
  3. Now put the 2lb beef into a bowl and take the mixed spices and mix together with the beef
  4. Dice onions take some honey and toss together then saute the oinions
  5. Dice the Mushrooms add a pinch of salt and perpper and saute the Mushrooms
  6. Take a stick of butter and put it on a hot pan and let melt once done toast the Buns then flatten out patty meat place onto pan an let cook
  7. once done take 2 pieces of cheese and place on the patties cover to let the cheese melt
  8. slice and rinse Lettuce
  9. Now put together spread the sauce on both sides of the bun then place in this order Lettuce, beef, Mushroom, Onion, and bun and enjoy
