Veggie Burger

This is a more healthier approach to a still a delicoius Burger

Ingredients

  • Veggie meat
  • Buns
  • salt & pepper
  • sriracha aioli
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Toast buns on a hot pan
  2. Grab 1 tbs of salt in pepper dumb into a bowl with the veggie meat and Mix
  3. Dice 2 pieces of Garlic and place on frying pan and let saute then make patties and place on the pan
  4. Take the sriracha aioli and spread on the top of the bun
  5. cut one piece of tomato, lettuce and onion then rinse
  6. Now place the lettuce at the bottom then the meat followed by tomato and onion topped of with the bun and enjoy.
